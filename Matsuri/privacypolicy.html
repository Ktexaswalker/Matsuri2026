Privacy policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this website (hereinafter referred to as “the Website”) collects, uses, and protects personal data, in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council (General Data Protection Regulation – GDPR), and applicable data protection laws.

By accessing and using this Website, users acknowledge that they have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for the processing of personal data collected through this Website is the organization responsible for the event and its official communication channels.

For any questions related to data protection, privacy, or the exercise of user rights, you may contact us through the contact information provided on the Website.

3. Personal Data Collected

This Website does not actively collect personal data through registration forms, user accounts, or third-party tracking systems.

However, personal data may appear on the Website in the following contexts:

  • Images and audiovisual material (photographs and videos) taken during public events.
  • Information related to participants, such as artists, performers, speakers, volunteers, collaborators, or sponsors.
  • Publicly available information provided voluntarily by the individuals involved in the event.

No sensitive personal data is intentionally collected or processed.

4. Images, Photography, and Audiovisual Content

During the event, photographs and videos may be taken for documentation, promotional, and communication purposes. These materials may be published on this Website and other official communication channels related to the event.

By attending the event, individuals acknowledge and accept that:

  • Their image may be captured incidentally or intentionally in public areas.
  • Such images may be used for informational, promotional, and archival purposes.
  • No commercial exploitation of individual images will take place beyond the promotion and communication of the event itself.

If any individual considers that their image has been published inappropriately or wishes to request its removal, they may contact the organization, and reasonable measures will be taken where applicable.

5. Purpose of Data Processing

Any personal data appearing on the Website is processed exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Promotion and documentation of the event.
  • Informational content related to participants and activities.
  • Communication with the public regarding past, current, or future editions of the event.

Personal data will not be used for profiling, automated decision-making, or commercial data trading.

6. Legal Basis for Processing

The legal basis for processing personal data includes:

  • Legitimate interest in promoting and documenting a public cultural or social event.
  • Consent, where applicable, provided explicitly or implicitly through participation in the event.
  • Public interest, when the event is open to the general public.

7. Data Retention

Personal data, including images and related content, will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

8. Data Sharing

The Website does not share personal data with third parties, except when legally required or when necessary for the technical operation of the Website (e.g., hosting services), under appropriate data protection safeguards.

No data is sold, transferred, or shared for commercial purposes.

9. User Rights

In accordance with the GDPR, individuals have the right to:

  • Access their personal data.
  • Request rectification or deletion of their data.
  • Object to or restrict the processing of their data.
  • Request the removal of images or content where legally applicable.

Requests must be submitted through the contact details provided on the Website and will be addressed within the legally established timeframes.

10. Intellectual Property and Content Rights

All content published on the Website, including but not limited to photographs, videos, graphic design, text, and website layout, is protected by intellectual property and copyright laws.

Unauthorized reproduction, distribution, or use of such content without prior consent is strictly prohibited.

11. Liability Disclaimer

The organization shall not be held responsible for:

  • The use of images or information by third parties without authorization.
  • Content shared externally beyond the control of the Website.
  • Incidental appearance of individuals in public event imagery.

The Website is provided “as is” and is intended solely for informational and promotional purposes.

12. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically.

13. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of personal data, please refer to the contact section of the Website.
